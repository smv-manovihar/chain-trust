# Products Management (Manufacturer)
Route: `/manufacturer/products`

## Layout Overview
- **Header**: Title "Products", count of active products, and "Add Product" button.
- **Product List**: A grid or list of enrolled products.
- **Product Cards**: Show Product Name, SKU/ID, Category, and Number of Batches.

## Interaction Guides
1. **Add Product (Plus Icon)**:
   - *Label*: "Add Product".
   - *Action*: Navigates to `/manufacturer/products/new` (the enrollment wizard).
   - *Position*: Top right header.
2. **View Details**:
   - *Action*: Click on a product card to see its history and associated batches.
